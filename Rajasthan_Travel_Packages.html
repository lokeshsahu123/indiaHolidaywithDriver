Frequently Asked Questions

The cost of a Rajasthan trip can vary widely. On average, a mid-range budget trip could range from RS 80000 to RS 15000 per person. Luxury trips may cost significantly more, while budget travelers can find cost-effective options.

The best time to visit Rajasthan is during the winter months, from October to March. The weather during this period is relatively cool, making it comfortable for exploring the vibrant cities and historical sites of Rajasthan.

The ideal duration for a Rajasthan trip depends on the places you want to visit and the experiences you seek. A well-rounded itinerary typically ranges from 7 to 14 days, allowing time to explore major cities like Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer, along with the surrounding attractions.

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is one of the most visited cities in the state. Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur is famous for its historical landmarks, including the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amer Fort. Its vibrant markets and cultural richness make it a top choice for tourists.

In many cases, traveling with a tour package can be cost-effective. Tour packages often include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and sometimes meals, providing a more streamlined and budget-friendly option compared to planning each component separately. Additionally, packages may offer discounts and negotiated rates, making them a convenient choice for travelers. With our package, you can travel at cheaper rates.

While there is no strict dress code, it's advisable to dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites. Lightweight, breathable fabrics such as cotton are ideal, given Rajasthan's warm climate. For women, long skirts or pants paired with tops that cover the shoulders are suitable. Men can opt for lightweight trousers and collared shirts. In some traditional or religious settings, it's customary to cover the head, so carrying a scarf or shawl is a good idea. Always check the specific guidelines for each location.
