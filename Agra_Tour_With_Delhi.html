2. Overview Of Your Agra Tour With Delhi

Delhi and Agra, located in North India, are captivating tourist destinations. From the iconic Taj Mahal in Agra to the vibrant streets of Delhi, these cities offer a splendid vacation experience. Our thoughtfully curated Taj Mahal Tour From Delhi packages make exploring these cities easy and convenient.

Delhi:
  • India Gate: Iconic war memorial and popular gathering spot.
  • Qutub Minar: UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring a towering minaret and ancient ruins.
  • Humayun's Tomb: Magnificent Mughal architecture surrounded by lush gardens.
  • Lotus Temple: Bahá'í : House of Worship is known for its striking lotus-shaped design.
  • Akshardham Temple: Intricate architecture, cultural exhibits, and a stunning water show.
Agra :
  • Taj Mahal: UNESCO World Heritage Site, the epitome of eternal love and architectural marvel.
  • Agra Fort: Impressive Mughal fortress offering panoramic views of the Taj Mahal.
  • Itimad-ud-Daulah's Tomb: Exquisite tomb, often called the "Baby Taj."
  • Mehtab Bagh: Riverside Park provides a picturesque view of the Taj Mahal.
  • Fatehpur Sikris: UNESCO-listed city with well-preserved Mughal architecture.
